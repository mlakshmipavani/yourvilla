Our website manages a real-estate agency. It acts as a platform for owners, tenants and other customers to unanimously connect with each other to buy, sell or rent properties such as houses, villas or apartment. Our customers get the opportunity to select a property that suite their requirements. With a wide range of property options our customers can select a property based on the area, number of BHK’s or based on range which they can afford easily.

Any user who wants to post his property for sale/rent on our website has to register and then login to our website. This eliminates the overhead of maintenance for the owner as it is taken care by our website. Thus it also benefits the tenants to raise a maintenance issue by just a click and our website’s representatives will take care of the rest.

With a variety of options to choose from, our general customers can find their dream house in just a click. We provide all the relevant and necessary details regarding the property such as the location with a Google map, amenities available, number of BHK’s, amount that the owner expects and also information on the places that are close by such as hospitals, grocery stores, schools, play homes and malls.

Once a customer registers as an owner he can post his property for sale/rent with the details of his house such as the area, number of bedrooms, furnished/semi furnished etc. The owner can also upload pictures of his property. If the owner is interested in renting his property to a tenant he can view details of payment done by the tenant like monthly rent, advance paid/due, lease amount. The owner can also specify if he wants to pay the maintenance or include it in the rent. We upload all the information such as the appliances that are provided by the owner for that particular property.

On the other hand the tenant has the privilege to view necessary details of the house before paying the advance and rent .If there is any problem with the appliances belonging to the house we will send plumbers or electricians to repair or the tenant also has the option to contact a plumber/electrician through our website.
